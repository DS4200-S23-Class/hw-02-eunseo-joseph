Task Abstraction

The low level action in this graph is looking specifically for the last three day's average maximum temperature in Vancouver. The medium level goal is to see how the temperature compares to other maximum temperatures over different months in Vancouver over the past 50 years. The high level action is to let the user look at previous temperature data and observe any trends that may be relevant in the data, and observe how their last 3 days compares to Vancouver's weather history.

The low level action in this graph would be to compare between Seattle and Portland. The medium level action in this graph would be to search specific times in Seattle or Portland where the temperatures are really above normal. The high level action would be to analyze the temperature readings on the graph and discover whether or not there is a trend between Seattle and Portland or between time and temperature. This makes the target of the graph to understand the relationship between temperature and time in two cities: Seattle and Portland.
